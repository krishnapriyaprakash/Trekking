    Camp Wanderers has been welcoming campers and travel-enthusiasts all over India since more than a decade. Our camps are not only about spending time with nature & yourself but creating incredible friendships that strengthen with time. Ask any of our campers and they will tell you, over and over, it is ‘the thing’, they’ve been waiting all year! Our camps guarantee an experience that will open your mind, connect you to an amazing community of adventure and travel enthusiasts and show you that a couple of days in the woods (unplugged from internet and mobile phones) can have a huge impact in your work and life, and increase your productivity, imagination, and, we think, Cheerfulness.

    The popular trek are.....

    Roopkund Trek (Uttarakhand)

    Famous for the mysterious skeleton lake at the top

    Manali to Leh Trek (Himachal Pradesh to Ladakh)

    A high-altitude trek that traverses the rugged mountains of the Indian Himalayas.

    Tarsar Marsar Trek (Kashmir)

    Known for its stunning alpine lakes and lush meadows.

    Chadar Trek (Ladakh)

    This unique winter trek takes you along the frozen Zanskar River, offering a once-in-a-lifetime experience in the coldest part of India.

    Markha Valley Trek (Ladakh)

    A moderate-to-difficult trek, this one offers a fascinating insight into Ladakhi culture

    Goecha La Trek (Sikkim)

    A challenging trek that provides spectacular views of the Kanchenjunga Range, India’s highest mountain.

    Singalila Ridge Trek (Sikkim)

    Offers incredible views of Mount Kanchenjunga and Mount Everest.

    Kalsubai Trek (Maharashtra)

    The highest peak in Maharashtra, this trek offers panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountain range.

    Rajmachi Trek (Maharashtra)

    A popular trek near Mumbai, the Rajmachi trek takes you through lush forests, waterfalls.
